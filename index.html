Introduction

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I am a biotechnology postgraduate with hands-on expertise in molecular biology, cell culture, and translational disease models. My research experience spans from bench-based techniques (RNA isolation, qPCR, immunoblotting, spheroid culture) to in vivo animal models for PCOS and endometriosis.

I am particularly interested in exploring the molecular mechanisms of reproductive disorders and their therapeutic targets, with a focus on endometriosis and ovarian disease models.

I also apply R programming for gene expression analysis, visualization, and statistical validation of experimental data. This integration of wet-lab and computational skills strengthens my ability to analyze and interpret biological research outcomes.
